If you are interested in our laboratory, please contact Prof. Nakata or our students and visit us!
We encourage you to visit first to see if our research topics and the laboratory's atmosphere are a good fit for you. Since we may not be able to accommodate unannounced visits, we recommend contacting us in advance to schedule a date and time. Furthermore, <span style="color: #e74c3c;">if you plan to apply to the graduate program with the Nakata Laboratory as your first choice, you must contact Prof. Nakata before submitting your application.</span> If you would like to consult <span style="color: #e74c3c;">with current students</span> regarding laboratory activities or entrance exam preparation, please feel free to reach out to the student email address.
